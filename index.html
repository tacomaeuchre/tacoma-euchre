Casual, Drop-in Euchre
Every other Wednesday at 7pm at Doyle's Public House
You can find us in "The Snug" (private room by the restrooms)

Founded by the six-time bracelet winner of the totally real World Series of Euchre, Euchre is now here in Tacoma. Ten years ago, he started the Seattle Euchre Club and it still rocks strong.
It's time to bring the fun and games to his new hometown, Tacoma.

Euchre players of all skill levels are welcome - Euchre is a splendid backdrop to socialize and enjoy the company of friends.
